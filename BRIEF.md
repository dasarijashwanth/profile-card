# Week 6: One-Page Portfolio (MMV)

## 1. The Contract (I/O)

**Input:** Name, 2-line bio, 3 proudest achievements, and one photo.

**Output:** A live, single-page website deployed to a real URL.

## 2. The Magical Moment

Instant digital presence ("I have a website I can text my mom!").

## 3. Scope Discipline (What we are NOT building)

One simple page. No custom domains, blog sections, contact forms, or animated themes.

## 4. Personality / Signature Detail

The challenge of self-reflection—finding the specific magical moment about yourself to put on the page.
